My favorite BBQ Recipe

(KABAAB) a middle eastern famous BBQ with a slightly deferent recipe

ingredients for Kabab

  • 2 pounds chicken breast undercut
  • 1/2 cup onion juice
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic paste
  • 6 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Salt and paper as needed
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  1. chop the chicken breast in cubes
  2. mix onion juice with lemon juice yogurt and garlic paste
  3. Marinate the chicken in this liquid and leave for 2 hours
  4. tread on skewers with the marinated beef
  5. Grill them and your Kabab is ready
